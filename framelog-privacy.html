Local-first · Your workout data stays on your device

This Privacy Policy describes how FrameLog ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles your information. FrameLog is developed and operated by Robert Helm, operating as North Star App Studio ("Developer," "we," "us"), located in the United States.

FrameLog is built on a local-first architecture. Your workout data — every set, rep, weight, note, personal record, and training history — is stored directly on your device. We do not operate servers that store your workout data. We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

By downloading or using FrameLog, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

The following information is created by you and stored locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage. It is never transmitted to us:

  • Workout data: Exercise names, sets, reps, weights, rest times, workout duration, workout notes, exercise notes
  • Training preferences: Workout split structure, exercise order, target sets and rep ranges, weak point selections
  • Profile data: Training goal, bodyweight, preferred weight unit, split type
  • Custom content: Custom exercise names and custom workout day names

This data is included in your device's standard iOS backup (iCloud or computer backup) if you have backups enabled. We have no access to it.

2. Information Processed by Third Parties

2.1 Subscription Management (RevenueCat)

FrameLog uses RevenueCat, Inc. to manage in-app subscriptions ("FrameLog Pro"). When you purchase, restore, or manage a subscription, RevenueCat processes:

  • A randomly generated app user identifier (not linked to your name)
  • Purchase and transaction events (which subscription, when, renewal status)
  • Basic device and platform information (device type, operating system, country)

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment card details. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy. We use this data solely to deliver and verify your subscription.

2.2 Payments (Apple)

All payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive, store, or process your payment card details, Apple ID, or billing address. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

2.3 App Distribution (Apple)

FrameLog is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect data related to your download and use of the App in accordance with Apple's own policies, outside of our control.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

FrameLog does not collect, and does not contain SDKs that collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number (except if you email us directly for support)
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) or cross-app tracking data
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, Firebase, or Meta SDK)
  • Browsing history

FrameLog does not track you across apps or websites, and does not request App Tracking Transparency permission because it performs no tracking.

4. Support Communications

If you email us for support, we receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to respond to you and to improve the App. We retain support emails for up to 12 months, then delete them. We do not share them with third parties.

5. How Your Information Is Used

Because your workout data never leaves your device, we cannot and do not use it for any purpose. Data processed by RevenueCat is used only to deliver your subscription. Support emails are used only to help you.

We never sell, rent, or trade any personal information.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Workout data: Stored on your device. Delete it anytime via Settings → Reset All Data, or by deleting the App (this permanently erases all local data).
  • Subscription data: Managed by RevenueCat and Apple. Cancelling your subscription stops future processing.
  • Support emails: Deleted within 12 months. Request earlier deletion by emailing us.

7. Data Security

Your workout data is stored in sandboxed app storage, inaccessible to other apps. The App does not request unnecessary permissions and makes no outbound network requests containing your personal workout data. However, no storage method is completely secure, and you are responsible for the physical security of your device. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

FrameLog is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. For users in the EU, the App is not directed at children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, email northstarappstudio@gmail.com and we will address it.

9. Your Rights — European Economic Area, UK (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to processing of your personal data. Because we store no workout data on our servers, the only personal data we may hold is support email communications and the limited subscription data described above.

Our legal basis for processing support emails is legitimate interest (responding to your request). Our legal basis for subscription data is performance of a contract (delivering your subscription). To exercise any right, email northstarappstudio@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Your Rights — California (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share your personal information. The only personal information we collect is your email (if you contact support) and the limited subscription identifiers processed by RevenueCat. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights. To make a request, email northstarappstudio@gmail.com.

11. Other Jurisdictions

If you use FrameLog from outside the United States, you understand your information (limited to the categories above) may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date and, where required, a notice in the App. If we add or change third-party services that process data, we will update this policy before that change takes effect. Continued use after changes means you accept them.
