This Privacy Policy describes how FrameLog ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") handles your information. FrameLog is developed and operated by Robert Helm, operating as Northstar App Studio ("Developer," "we," "us"), located in the United States.

FrameLog is built on a local-first architecture. Your workout data — including sets, reps, weights, notes, personal records, and training history — is stored directly on your device. We do not operate servers that store your workout history. We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

By downloading or using FrameLog, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

The following information is created by you and stored locally on your device, using standard on-device app storage. It is not uploaded to FrameLog servers:

  • Workout data: exercise names, sets, reps, weights, rest times, workout duration, workout notes, and exercise notes
  • Training preferences: workout split structure, exercise order, target sets and rep ranges, and weak point selections
  • Profile data: training goal, bodyweight, preferred weight unit, and split type
  • Custom content: custom exercise names and custom workout day names

This data may be included in your device's standard iOS backup, such as iCloud Backup or a computer backup, depending on your device settings. We do not have access to your local workout history.

2. Information Processed by Third Parties

2.1 Subscription Management — RevenueCat

FrameLog uses RevenueCat, Inc. to manage in-app subscriptions for FrameLog Pro. When you purchase, restore, or manage a subscription, RevenueCat may process limited subscription-related information, including:

  • A randomly generated app user identifier or subscription identifier
  • Purchase and transaction events, such as subscription type, purchase date, renewal status, and entitlement status
  • Basic device, app, platform, and country information used to support subscription functionality

RevenueCat does not receive your workout history from FrameLog. RevenueCat does not receive your payment card details from us. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy. We use RevenueCat only to deliver, verify, restore, and manage FrameLog Pro subscription access.

2.2 Payments — Apple

All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, store, or process your payment card details, Apple ID password, or billing address. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

2.3 App Distribution — Apple

FrameLog is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect data related to your download, purchase, subscription, and use of the App in accordance with Apple's own policies, outside of our control.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

FrameLog does not collect, and does not include third-party SDKs that collect, the following categories from your workout usage:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number, except if you email us directly for support
  • Precise location data
  • Advertising identifiers, IDFA, or cross-app tracking data
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Third-party advertising or analytics data
  • Browsing history

FrameLog does not track you across apps or websites and does not request App Tracking Transparency permission because it does not perform advertising tracking.

4. Support Communications

If you email us for support, we receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use this information only to respond to you, provide support, investigate bugs, and improve the App. We retain support emails for up to 12 months, then delete them unless we need to keep them longer for legal, security, or dispute-resolution reasons.

5. How Your Information Is Used

Because your workout history is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to FrameLog servers, we cannot view or analyze your workout history.

Limited subscription-related information processed by RevenueCat and Apple is used only to deliver, verify, restore, and manage FrameLog Pro. Support emails are used only to respond to support requests and improve the App.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Workout data: Stored locally on your device. You can delete it using the reset option in Settings or by deleting the App. This permanently removes local data from your device.
  • Subscription data: Managed by RevenueCat and Apple according to their retention policies. Cancelling your subscription stops future subscription renewals but may not immediately delete historical transaction records required for billing, accounting, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
  • Support emails: Retained for up to 12 months unless we need to keep them longer for legal, security, or dispute-resolution reasons. You may request earlier deletion by emailing us.

7. Data Security

Your workout data is stored in sandboxed app storage on your device, which is designed to prevent other apps from accessing it. FrameLog does not request unnecessary permissions and does not make outbound network requests containing your personal workout history.

However, no storage or transmission method is completely secure. You are responsible for the physical security of your device and your device backup settings. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

FrameLog is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. For users in the European Union, the App is not directed at children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, email northstarappstudio@gmail.com and we will address it.
